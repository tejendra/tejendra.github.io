I’ve ran five marathons so far:

1. **Marine Corps Marathon** — Washington, D.C. area  
2. **Walt Disney World Marathon** — Orlando  
3. **Publix Atlanta Marathon** — Atlanta  
4. **Vermont City Marathon** — Burlington  
5. **Chicago Marathon** — Chicago  

Each one taught me something different about pacing, training, and showing up on the day. I’ll add a longer post later with a bit about each race and what stuck with me.

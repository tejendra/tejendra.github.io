I received a 1-star challenge coin from Brigadier General Korey Amundson for my work during the Balikatan Exercise in the Philippines.

Balikatan is an annual joint military exercise between the Philippines and the United States, focused on interoperability, humanitarian assistance, and disaster response. Contributing to that mission and being recognized with a challenge coin from the general was an honor—a small token that carries a lot of meaning for the work the team did there.

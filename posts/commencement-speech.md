In 2015 I had the honor of giving the commencement speech for the Computer Engineering graduating class at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth.

It was a chance to speak to my peers—and to the families in the room—about what we’d built during our time there and what comes next. I’m grateful to the department and my classmates for the opportunity.

The full speech is on YouTube. If you’re a current or former UMass Dartmouth engineer, or anyone curious what a computer engineering grad had to say at the podium that day, here it is.

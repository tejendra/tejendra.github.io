Just got back from an amazing vacation with hundreds of stunning photos? Don't let WhatsApp compress your memories into pixelated mush.

**PicStream** is a web app I'm working on so you can share those crystal-clear vacation pics and videos in their full, glorious original quality with friends and family. No more trading resolution for convenience.

## Why I built it

Most messaging apps compress images and video heavily to save bandwidth. That's fine for quick selfies, but trip photos—sunsets, landscapes, group shots you'll look at for years—deserve to stay sharp. I wanted a simple way to upload once and share a link where people can view or download the originals, without signing up for a heavyweight service.

## What it does

- Upload photos and videos from your trip
- Share a single link with whoever you want
- Viewers get full-quality files—no re-encoding, no shrinking
- No account required to view (upload may require sign-in, depending on how you run it)

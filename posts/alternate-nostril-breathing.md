**Kapalbhati** is a small web app that guides you through rapid, forced exhalations and passive inhalations, known as skull-shining breathing exercise.

**Anulom Vilom** is a small web app that guides you through alternate nostril breathing (anulom vilom) with clear timing for each phase.

## Why I built it

I practice pranayama in the morning and wanted a metronome like cue for how long to inhale and exhale. I had been using a YouTube playlist, but ads would interrupt in the middle of a session. I vibe coded simple apps so the rhythm stays steady and predictable.

## What it does

### Kapalbhati

- **Pick a pace.** Start with 1 exhale per second and as you advance, you can increase up to 2 exhales per second.

### Anulom Vilom

- **Pick a focus.** *Athletic* emphasizes rhythm and control you can use while moving, without extreme breath holds. *Stillness* uses slow, smooth timing for calm and quiet awareness. *CO₂* leans on longer exhales and holds to build comfort with higher carbon dioxide levels. You can also follow a gradual progression instead of locking into one mode.
- **Pick a pattern.** Inhale/exhale only, or add holds: inhale–hold–exhale, inhale–exhale–hold, or the full inhale–hold–exhale–hold cycle.
- **Set phase lengths** and start. For fifteen minutes, the app walks you through each phase of the pattern you chose.
